Banana Pudding

Banana Pudding is Southern dish consisting of layers of wafers, banana, and custard.
Sometimes it is topped with whipped cream. It can be served hot or cold.
It's my favorite dessert, and it is easy to make, while being cost effective.
I remember making it with my friend in my second year of college. It was very easy and fun to make.
It has been my favorite dessert ever since.

Ingredients Quantity
Pudding Mix 1 Box
Vanilla Extract 1 TSP
Bananas 1-2
Sweetened Condensed Milk 2 Cups
Whipped Cream User Judgement
Vanilla Wafers 1 Box
